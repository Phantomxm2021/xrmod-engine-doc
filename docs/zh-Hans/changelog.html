2.0.0-pre.1

2022年8月4日

The XRMOD engine provides a solution for implementing augmented reality functions on the cross platform. Embedding XRMOD engine on the cross platform does not need to change your original business and code, and only needs to call several APIs to complete the embedding. XRMOD engine is a free and without watermark metaverse construction system that supports commercial licenses (free commercial licenses).
